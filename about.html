How this began..

I've been a drummer and percussionist for 27 years, and have the passion to show others how fun this instrument can be. I developed an enthusiasm for software and web development about two years ago. I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp to learn more about building websites. I want this site to be a great resource for all drummers and percussionists whether they're beginners or seasoned professionals.
